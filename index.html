WHAT
IS A
TO-DO LIST ?

Its is a list of task which one wants to accomplish.
For instance: No, I'm yet to buy the gift but it's on my To-Do List

A To-Do List gives opportunity to arrange your tasks with the most important task at the top

With a To-Do List, you can prioritize the things you need to do, keep track of what still needs to be finised.

  • With the application of Javascript, I built a To-Do List Web App
  • Save the page for offline use
  • Highlight accomplished tasked with a click
  • Remove a task by using the delete button at the right side
